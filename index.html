2024 UPENN ESAP

Nanotechnology & Device Fabrication

Nanoscience Cleanroom ISO-5 Lithography

An intensive research fellowship focused on the fabrication and characterization of nanoscale devices. Worked within a Class 100 cleanroom environment to process silicon wafers using advanced lithography techniques.

  • Synthesized gold nanoparticles and analyzed optical properties using spectroscopy.
  • Utilized Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM) and Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM) for surface characterization.
  • Developed protocols for thin-film deposition to control material properties at the atomic level.
2023 LBNL

Indoor Air Quality Sensing

Environmental Eng. Data Analysis Python

Conducted field research at Lawrence Berkeley National Labs to evaluate low-cost sensor performance in residential environments. The study aimed to improve ventilation strategies for energy-efficient buildings.

  • Engineered a portable data logging system using microcontrollers to monitor CO2 and PM2.5.
  • Wrote Python scripts to process large datasets, identifying sensor drift and correlation with reference instruments.
  • Presented statistical findings on pollutant accumulation dynamics to the senior research team.
